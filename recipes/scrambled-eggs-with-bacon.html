Scrambled eggs with bacon

This is not just scrambled eggs with bacon on the side. This is scrambled eggs with bacon cooked together! I think theyâ€™re a match made in breakfast heaven.

This easy breakfast meal is ready in just 10 minutes. Depending on the amount of servings you need, feel free to double or even triple it! Serve with waffle iron hash browns or stovetop hash browns for an amazing breakfast!

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • milk
  • salt and pepper
  • butter (to prevent sticking on the pan)
  • bacon

Instructions

  1. Cook 2 pieces of bacon according to the package directions, either skillet or the microwave. You can use regular bacon or turkey bacon if you want something lower in calories. Once cooked cut it up into 1/2 inch pieces.
  2. Crack eggs in a mixing bowl and add in the milk, salt, pepper and cooked bacon. Whisk together until eggs are beaten.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium high heat. Once melted add in eggs.
  4. Once the egg mixture seems to have set, break it up with a spatula to scramble and flip. Continue cooking and scrambling until eggs are fully cooked and no longer runny. Serve immediately and enjoy!
